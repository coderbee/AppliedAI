2022

Text-to-Image Generation Breakthrough

  • DALL·E 2 introduced by OpenAI in April, with over 1.5 million beta testers.
  • Stable Diffusion released by Stability AI, attracting over $100 million in funding.
  • Adobe, Getty Images, and Shutterstock integrated image generation into their products.

AI-Powered Code Generation

  • AlphaCode by DeepMind outperformed more than half of human contestants in coding competitions.
  • GitHub Copilot, an AI-powered code autocomplete system, became widely available.

Vision Transformers (ViTs) Evolution

  • Researchers combined self-attention mechanisms with convolutional neural networks.
  • ViTs adapted for video frame generation and 3D scene reconstruction.

Language Model Extensions

  • AI21 Labs' Jurassic-X introduced fact-checking modules.
  • Meta's Atlas capable of retrieving information from document databases.
  • OpenAI's ChatGPT fine-tuned to minimize untruthful or harmful outputs.

Multi-Task Models

  • Google’s PaLM showed state-of-the-art results in few-shot learning across hundreds of language tasks.
  • DeepMind's Gato learned over 600 diverse tasks.
  • Google’s RT-1 enabled robots to perform over 700 tasks with impressive zero-shot performance.

2023

Generative AI Explosion

  • ChatGPT and similar models gained widespread adoption.
  • Major tech companies launched their own chatbots and text generators.
  • Open-source alternatives like LLaMA, Falcon, and Mistral emerged.

Multimodal Models

  • GPT-4 integrated DALL-E 3 for image generation and interpretation.
  • Google introduced Gemini, processing mixed inputs of text, images, audio, and video.

AI in Hollywood

  • Writers and actors strikes over AI usage in film and television industry.
  • Agreements reached to limit AI’s use in replacing human creativity.

Data and Copyright Challenges

  • Lawsuits filed against AI companies over training data usage.
  • Some platforms began charging for data access or forbidding AI training on their content.

Regulatory Responses

  • EU worked on the AI Act.
  • China implemented regulations on AI-generated media and face recognition.
  • US took steps to promote individual privacy and civil rights in AI development.

AI-Generated Music

  • AI helped produce a new Beatles single.
  • Voice cloning technologies emerged for music production.

2024

Agentic AI Systems

  • Improved abilities in reasoning, tool use, and desktop application control.
  • Introduction of tools and frameworks for building agentic workflows (e.g., Autogen, AG2, CrewAI, LangGraph).
  • Major companies like Microsoft, Meta, and OpenAI launched agentic AI solutions.

Price Reductions in AI Services

  • Dramatic decrease in the cost of accessing state-of-the-art AI models.
  • Some companies cut per-token prices by nearly 90 percent.

Generative Video Advancements

  • Introduction of powerful text-to-video, image-to-video, and video-to-video generators.
  • Notable examples: OpenAI's Sora, Runway's Gen 3 Alpha, Adobe's Firefly Video, Meta's Movie Gen.

Smaller, More Efficient Models

  • Shift towards smaller language models capable of running on smartphones.
  • Techniques like knowledge distillation, parameter pruning, and quantization improved efficiency.

Novel Partnership Arrangements

  • Major tech companies formed partnerships with AI startups instead of traditional acquisitions.
  • Examples: Microsoft with Inflection AI, Amazon with Adept AI and Covariant, Google with Character.ai.
